ABOUT AHLUL-KHAIR LABORATORIES

The AML is located on the Kamshat filling staion, Wudil Town, Wudil LGA, Kano State of Nigeria. The AML aims to provide high quality healthcare for both private and public hospitals, with the advanced medical facilities, to all of our patients in a pleasant and comfortable environment.

Mission
AML is committed to providing the highest quality laboratory diagnosis for the community and patient welfare services.




Vision
AML aims to be a national renowned medical lab centre providing highest quality medical laboratory healthcare services in the diagnosis of diseases according to national and International Standards.

AML History


The group patriotic citizens of Wudil with the approval of the ministry of Health, Kano state, Ahlul-Khair Medical laboratories was established in 2011 in order to meet the increasing medical needs from the local population. This diagnostic centre provided a great opportunity of medical laboratory clinical practice for patient care service to curtail high demands and support government effort for the general population of wudil local government. Through six years of the hard work of our staff, the Ahlul-khair Medical laboratory has gradually grown into today's giant diagnostic centre for patient’s welfare, training and research activities. In the early days, the AML centre was located in the Wudil town, Kamshat filling station, Block A occupying four rooms.

Client Charter
We pledge to:
• Provide quality, friendly services, regardless of race, religion, age, sex and socioeconomic status.
• Provide accurate information on the services, facilities, important aspects of diagnosis for treatment and the prevention of disease, where applicable.
• Provide immediate care to those termed as ‘emergency’ as determined by the triage system.
• Protect the safety and dignity of clients during provision of services, and to maintain client’s confidentiality except to those authorized.
• Provide clean, comfortable and safe facilities and environment.
• Accept referral from other private and public health facilities whenever possible.
• Refer patient to other diagnostic facilities, when necessary.
• Be prepared to listen, accept clients’ complaints, and take the necessary measures immediately.
• Provide clients with choices to accept services, after explaining the implications.
• Conduct clinical medical laboratory diagnosis using standard operational procedures (SOPs) and based on guidelines of medical ethics.
